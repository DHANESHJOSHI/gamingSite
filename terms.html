Eligibility

The "WinGame" Contest Is Open To Individuals Who Are 18 Years Of Age Or Older And Who Are Residents Of India.

Contest Rules and Gameplay

  • Each contest will last for 15 minutes.
  • There will be a total of 28 different quizzes in each contest, each consisting of two questions, A and B. Each quiz will be displayed for 30 seconds, and the answer options will be displayed after 20 seconds. The final quiz in each contest will have its options revealed after 20 seconds, creating excitement for players.
  • The number of options for each quiz may vary, and points will be scored according to the following table:
  • 3 options: 1 point
  • 5 options: 2 points
  • 7 options: 3 points
  • 10 options: 5 points
  • Points will be allocated based on the combination of answers to questions A and B in each quiz. For example, if the answer to question A is option 3, and the answer to question B is option 5, the combination becomes 35, and the points will be awarded accordingly.
  • The combination with the highest point total after the last quiz will determine the winner
  • Player can purchase minimum quantity of 1 = Rs.2 to 300 = Rs.600 on min one answer combination.
